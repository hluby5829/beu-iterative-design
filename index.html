BeU Iterative Design

BeU delivery is an on-demand food delivery service in Africa similar to DoorDash or UberEats. They are the #1 food delivery service in Ethiopia, 67% cheaper than the competition, with plans to expand their services to new markets and eventually become the super APP of Africa.

Our group is designing a mobile interface for beU because we felt that a lot of users would be interacting with the application on their phones as is typical from DoorDash and UberEats users. Users who would be impacted are those with slow wifi speeds or low data left in their monthly plans. PLEASE ADD MORE RORISENNA!!

Visual Style Guide

Next, I moved on to a redesign of buttons, icons, and the navbar. Many of the original buttons were hard to find and didn't offer much in the form of text color contrast. The original navbar gave no indication of which tab you were in, now the current tab is highlighted. I also fiddled around with the idea of incorporating a light and dark mode toggle, which is common for modern websites.

Hifi-Wireframes

With these designs in mind, I was able to create the components for my Hifi-Prototypes of the site on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Since all sections of the design follow a scrolling layout, I decided to use Bootstrap grid instead of flex box. This allows us to easily set built in break points for different screen sizes.
